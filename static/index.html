Below, enter the username given to you. Before pressing the button to start, make sure that you are ready to listen to sound from the device. To begin listening, press the play button to start the test. You may only listen to the playback once.

The test will consist of listening to a sentence spoken above background noise you might hear in a restaurant. The goal is to understand the sentence said by the main person speaking. After the audio clip finishes, the microphone will turn green and remove the cross, indicating that you should say the sentence that you heard in the clip. Green volume bars above the microphone indicate the input volume.

When you are done recording, press the "Next Audio" button to play the next clip. The recording will end automatically after 5 seconds of silence.
